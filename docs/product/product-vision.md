# Product Vision & Business Context

## ?? Vision
CoreSupply aims to revolutionize the B2B industrial supply chain by providing a **resilient, cloud-native platform** that connects manufacturers with suppliers in real-time.

## ?? Target Audience (Personas)
*   **Hans (Procurement Manager):** Needs reliability, speed, and audit logs.
*   **Supplier System (Machine):** Needs high-throughput APIs.

## ?? KPIs (Success Metrics)
*   **Availability:** 99.99% uptime during business hours.
*   **Latency:** API response time < 200ms (p95).
*   **Throughput:** Support 10,000 orders/minute.
